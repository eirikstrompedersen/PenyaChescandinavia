Become member

Penya Chescandinavia is an official Valencia CF penya. We are very happy that you have chosen to support the club and us! With your membership fee you contribute to strengthen the club and Chescandinavia in the effort to spread the interest for the club. You also help make sure that the voice of the supporters abroad is being heard at the club.

All nationalities are welcome to join of course! Your membership will support the Penya work to spread Valencianism across the Nordic countries and online. We aim to keep you informed about what is happening at the club and we are of course happy to help with any queries you might have about travelling to Valencia. We arrange meet ups both in Valencia and in the Nordic countries. Your membership also gives you voting rights at our general assembly You contribute to maintaining our web site and making possible the sharing of articles and knowledge about Valencia CF There are some additional rights here.

Choose an adult membership (Vuxen) if you are 18 or over or a Youth membership (Ungdom) if the member is younger. You will be redirected to paypal to complete the membership fee payment. Do not forget to mention your birth date in the commentary section to enable us to get your membership card from Valencia CF.(ex. 1995-12-24).

After paying you will see a confirmation page from paypal. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to ask on penya@chescandinavia.com

For only €10 you become member for 1 year, and support the work done by Penya Chescandinavia.
